Classic Beef Burger

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 egg
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cheese, lettuce, tomato, buns

Instructions

  1. Mix beef with egg, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form patties and grill for 4–5 mins each side.
  3. Assemble with cheese, lettuce, and tomato on buns.
